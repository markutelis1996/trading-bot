# Trading Strategy — Bull

*Last updated: 2026-04-19 (initial setup)*

## Thesis
Beat S&P 500 through fundamentals-driven, low-frequency swing trades. Quality companies, catalysts, patience. No day trading.

## Buy Signals (need 2+ before entering)
1. **Earnings surprise:** beat consensus by >5% with raised guidance
2. **Sector tailwind:** strong sector momentum (>5% sector ETF move in 30 days)
3. **Insider buying:** C-suite or 10%+ holders buying on open market
4. **Relative strength:** stock up >10% while sector/market flat
5. **Fundamental catalyst:** new product launch, contract win, regulatory approval
6. **Oversold quality:** strong company down >15% on non-fundamental news

## Sell Signals (trigger immediate exit)
1. **Stop hit:** -7% from entry or 10% trailing stop triggered
2. **Thesis broken:** original buy reason no longer valid
3. **Earnings miss:** quarterly miss with lowered guidance
4. **Sector reversal:** clear sector top/breakdown
5. **Better opportunity:** capital needed for stronger setup

## Position Sizing
- Default: 3% of portfolio per position
- High conviction: 5% max
- Never >5% in any single position
- Cash target: 10-30% during normal conditions, 30-50% during uncertainty

## Universe
- US equities only
- Market cap >$2B (no micro caps)
- Average volume >1M shares/day
- No OTC, no pink sheets, no recent IPOs (<6 months)

## Blacklist (DO NOT TRADE)
- Meme stocks (GME, AMC, BBBY class)
- Crypto-related stocks during speculation phases
- Biotech binaries (FDA decision pending)
- Chinese ADRs (regulatory risk)

## Preferred Sectors (rotate as conditions change)
- Tech (quality compounders)
- Healthcare (non-binary)
- Industrials (cycle-aware)
- Consumer staples (defensive sleeve)
